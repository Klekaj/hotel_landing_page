Welcome to Chris Hotel

Few About Us

Set in Ksamil, less than 1 km from Puerto Rico Beach, Hotel Chris offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a garden. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a desk and free WiFi.
